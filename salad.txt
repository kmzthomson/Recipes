Spicy chicken salad

Ingredients

    100g/3½oz roasted chicken breast, roughly chopped
    1 celery stick, thinly sliced
    1 spring onion, thinly sliced
    25g/1oz red pepper, thinly sliced
    25g/1oz fennel, thinly sliced

For the dressing

    1 tbsp sweet chilli sauce
    2 tsp white wine vinegar
