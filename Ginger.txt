Ginger biscuits

Ingredients

    70g/2½oz butter
    80g/2¾oz light muscovado sugar
    2 tbsp black treacle or molasses
    8 cardamon pods
    250g/9oz plain flour
    ½ tsp bicarbonate of soda
    2 tsp ground cinnamon
    1 tsp ground ginger
    1 free-range egg, yolk only
    around 2 tbsp milk
    1 tbsp icing sugar
    1 lemon, juice only
